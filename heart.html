About Dr. Kunal Sarkar

Dr. Kunal Sarkar is one of the most well-known Cardiac Surgeons of Kolkata, Senior Vice Chairman, Director and Head of Cardiac Surgery at Medica Superspecialty Hospital. Dr. Sarkar practices coronary surgery as his pursuance of passion. He has the distinction of performing over 18,000 heart surgeries
